About

Welcome!

I am a Postdoc researcher at BIMSA Virginia Tech. I work in the Machine Learning Laboratory, advised by Bert Huang. I am also a member of the Discovery Analytics Center (DAC) at Virginia Tech. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from American University of Nigeria.

Research Interests

My research include the string theory with special focus on quantum computation and machine learning. The aim of my work is to find better ways to obtain MTC from 6d N=(1,0) superconformal field theories.
